Bethesda Game Studios

Bethesda Game Studios is the award-winning development team known around the world for their groundbreaking work on The Elder Scrolls and Fallout series. With titles like The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion, Fallout 3, The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim, and Fallout 4, they have consistently delivered exceptional gaming experiences.

Starfield, their first new universe in 25 years, garnered 10 million players in less than three weeks of its release, making it the biggest launch in the studio's history.
